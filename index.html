About TJ NETWORK

TJ Network Inc. is the Philippines’ newest broadcasting company. Also known as the Katropa Network, TJ Network brings superior Entertainment and responsible, unbiased, and timely delivery of comprehensive and accurate News and Information to Filipinos anywhere in the world through its TV, radio, online platforms, and new media.

Corporate Purpose

We enrich the lives of Filipinos everywhere with superior Entertainment and the responsible, unbiased, and timely delivery of accurate News and Information.

Corporate Vision

We are the respected, undisputed in the Philippine broadcast industry and the recognized media innovator and pacesetter in Asia. We are the Filipinos favorite tv network. We are a key partner in promoting the best in the Filipino.

Corporate Values

We place God above all. We uphold Integrity and Transparency. We are driven by our Passion for Excellence. We strive for Efficiency in everything we do. We pursue Creativity and Innovation.
